Publications

Auxetic Dihedral Escher Tessellations
Xiaokang Liu, Lin Lu, Lingxin Cao, Oliver Deussen, Changhe Tu
Graphical Models (presented on CVM 2024) April 2024
A framework to optimize dual-shape tessellations into deployable rotating semi-rigid structures while maintaining shape similarity by introducing a metric function that simultaneously considers rotational degrees of freedom and shape semantics, along with a two-step deployable optimization algorithm.

Waiting Time Prediction for Bottom-up Vat Photopolymerization
Lingxin Cao, Lin Lu, Xiaokang Liu, Jian Zhang, Tengfei Jiang, Changhe Tu, Baoquan Chen
Additive Manufacturing July 2023
A novel waiting time prediction method for bottom-up vat photopolymerization (WTP-VP) that uses a prediction model based on the concept of a prediction feature region. This method is suitable for actual production in the industry as it predicts waiting time in real-time for contours with complex topologies and requires less data than machine learning using per-layer mask images directly.

Fabricable Multi-Scale Wang Tiles
Xiaokang Liu, Chenran Li, Lin Lu, Oliver Deussen, Changhe Tu
Computer Graphics Forum (presented on SGP 2022) October 2022
A method to generate fabricable multi-scale Wang Tiles by utilizing a parametric model and a combination tiles generation method to create representative sets of multi-scale tiles. These sets effectively cover a wide range of densities while minimizing the number of elements, and employs a hierarchical strategy to quickly and accurately fill a given density field.

Fabricable Dihedral Escher Tessellations
Xiaokang Liu, Lin Lu, Andrei Sharf, Xin Yan, Dani Lischinski, Changhe Tu
CAD 2020 (Presented on SPM 2020, Best Paper Award, 1st Place) June 2020
A method for generating dual-shape tessellations. Given an input image, search for a dual image in the graphic library and optimize both simultaneously to create "mutual-surrounding" dual patterns, which are introduced onto a 3D surface, innovatively generating perforated surface model.
